Introduction

Welcome to the Computer Engineering Department at MH Saboo Siddik Polytechnic. Our department offers high-quality education and training in computer engineering, equipping students with the skills and knowledge needed for successful careers in the field. Computer engineering (CoE or CpE) is a branch of computer science and electronic engineering that integrates several fields of computer science and electronic engineering required to develop computer hardware and software.[1] Computer engineering is referred to as computer science and engineering at some universities.
Computer engineers require training in electronic engineering, computer science, hardware-software integration, software design, and software engineering. It uses the techniques and principles of electrical engineering and computer science, and can encompass areas such as artificial intelligence (AI), robotics, computer networks, computer architecture and operating systems. Computer engineers are involved in many hardware and software aspects of computing, from the design of individual microcontrollers, microprocessors, personal computers, and supercomputers, to circuit design. This field of engineering not only focuses on how computer systems themselves work, but also on how to integrate them into the larger picture.[2] Robotics are one of the applications of computer engineering.
Computer engineering usually deals with areas including writing software and firmware for embedded microcontrollers, designing VLSI chips, analog sensors, mixed signal circuit boards, and operating systems. Computer engineers are also suited for robotics research, which relies heavily on using digital systems to control and monitor electrical systems like motors, communications, and sensors.

What You Will Learn

Focused Learning Areas:

  1. Fundamental Concepts: Understand basics like digital electronics, computer architecture, data structures, algorithms, and programming languages like C, C++, and Java.
  2. Hardware and Software: Gain skills in designing, analyzing, and implementing computer hardware components and software applications, including operating systems and software engineering principles.
  3. Programming and Development: Develop proficiency in programming languages and software development tools, focusing on designing, developing, and debugging software applications and systems.
  4. Networking and Communication: Learn about computer networks, network protocols, routing algorithms, network security, and communication technologies.
  5. Embedded Systems and IoT: Study embedded systems design and programming, including applications in the Internet of Things (IoT) such as sensor networks and smart devices.
  6. Data Management and Analysis: Gain knowledge of databases, data management techniques, SQL programming, data modeling, and data visualization techniques.
  7. Cybersecurity: Understand cybersecurity principles, threat analysis, encryption techniques, access control mechanisms, and security policies.
  8. Project Work and Practical Experience: Engage in hands-on projects, labs, internships, and industry projects to apply theoretical concepts in real-world scenarios and gain practical experience.

Overall, our program prepares you for a successful career in computer engineering by providing a comprehensive education and practical skills aligned with industry demands.
