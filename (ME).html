Introduction

Mechanical engineering is one of the oldest branches with a wide scope in design, analysis, testing, manufacturing, and maintenance of systems with moving parts. Our Diploma in Mechanical Engineering program aims to foster innovation and problem-solving skills.

You Will Learn:

  • Basics of thermal power plants
  • Inspection of manufactured components
  • CNC operating
  • Product design on a solid modeling package
  • Supervising production and maintenance of refrigeration and air conditioning systems

Employment Opportunities:

  • Machine tools industry/Manufacturing industry
  • Automobile industry
  • Thermal plants/Hydraulic power plants
  • Railways
  • Public sector companies
  • Army, Navy, Airforce

Our Laboratories:

The department is equipped with fully-fledged laboratories and workshops as per the syllabus of MSBTE:

  • Metrology and Quality Control Lab
  • Engineering Mechanics Lab
  • CNC Lab
  • Industrial and Fluid Power Lab
  • Refrigerator and Air Conditioning Lab
  • Theory of Machine Lab
  • Mechanical Machine Lab

Vision

To be the most sought-after department in the state of Maharashtra, recognized for an excellent model for technical education at the national and international levels.

Mission

To ensure an excellent model for technical education by continuously upgrading the quality of staff, facilities, and creating a vibrant ambiance for learning. We aim to develop engineers with skills, knowledge, and high ethical values who will be assets to society and the nation.

Elements of Mission Statement:

  1. To impart quality education so that our students shine in the field of engineering.
  2. To provide facilities, equipment, machinery, and ambiance to nurture tomorrow's leaders in engineering.
  3. To promote higher education and entrepreneurship.
  4. To encourage the upgradation of faculty qualification and skills of laboratory assistants through regular training to enhance teaching skills.
  5. To create a reward mechanism and ensure lifelong learning.
