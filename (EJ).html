The program in Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering aims at training diploma engineers to select, operate, and maintain the communication facilities - and other related electronics systems - which are today being put in place in the government, semi-government, and private sectors, besides guiding them in the self-employment sector. We endeavor to enable our students to make use of opportunities in the fields of electronic exchanges, optical fiber lines satellite links, microwave links, mobile links, and other infrastructure, ranging to VLSI technologies. This branch of Engineering has a key place in the field of computers, information technology, Electrical. power system operations, communication system, etc.

Er. Abdul Sayeed
Head of Department
The Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering was established in the year 2008 with an intake of 60 students. The main goal of the department is to provide students with quality education and continuous up-skilling so that they can succeed in the rapidly changing industry landscape. In addition to strong academic input, students are required to undertake projects and gain hands-on experience with both hardware and software tools. Industry visits and expert lectures from academia and industry professionals further enhance the learning experience. The students of the Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering department demonstrate outstanding academic performance, securing top university ranks and earning accolades for their achievements. They actively participate in national and international technical competitions, hackathons, and paper presentations, showcasing their skills and knowledge. The department encourages and supports students in presenting their work at conferences. Overall, it seems like the EXTC Department at MHSSCE is flourishing and making significant strides in research, academics, and extracurricular activities.

You Will Learn

  • To fabricate and test electronics circuits.
  • Manufacturing, testing, and maintenance of electronic devices and systems.
  • To understand lines communication, audio and video communication, and microwave communication.

Employment Opportunities

  • Electronic Exchanges, Department of Telecommunications, Doordarshan Parsar Bharti, BSNL, Railways, Electricity Board are major employers of Electronic Engineers.
  • Industries manufacturing Electronics & Communication systems, PCB components, Computers, and other electronic equipments.
  • Commercial organization providing services in repair & maintenance of Electronics & Computers.
  • Marketing and Sales Executives.

Laboratories

The department is having the following laboratories for practicals. All laboratories are fully equipped with testing & measuring with requires trainer kits with more specialized kits like microwave bench trainer, color TV trainer kit, and antenna & wave propagation kit to give clear practical vision of the theoretical principles.

  • Basic Electronics lab
  • Measurement & Microcontroller Lab
  • Linear Integrated Circuits Lab
  • Microprocessor & Microcontroller lab
  • Digital Electronics lab
  • Television Engineering Lab
  • Communication Lab
  • Industrial Electronic Lab

VISION

To produce creative and competent Electronics & Telecommunication engineers with high ethical standards and prepare them to excel in technical education to meet the requirements of industries at national and international levels.

MISSION

  1. To create and maintain an environment of academic freedom ensuring the exchange of ideas and dissemination of knowledge through effective teaching.
  2. To infuse scientific temperament among students.
  3. To develop practical skills through vigorous training in well-equipped laboratories and dedicated comprehensive teaching.
  4. To become a center of holistic development for students through curricular, co-curricular, and extra-curricular activities.
  5. To inculcate ethical values and sense of responsibilities towards nation, society, and environment.
